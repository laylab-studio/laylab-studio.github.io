About LayLab

LayLab delivers in-game overlays for Predecessor, CS2, and League of Legends, helping you stay prepared, track real-time stats, and avoid alt-tabbing. Built for gamers, by gamers.

Our team of passionate gamers and developers created LayLab to solve the common problem of needing to alt-tab during intense gaming sessions to access critical information.
